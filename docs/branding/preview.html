wflow logo concepts

Three takes on a stepped-w mark. All three reuse the brand palette (amber on near-black) so the chrome doesn't have to change. Each pairs an icon and a wordmark; pick a direction and we'll iterate.

A · Stepped valleys

Strict geometry. Four straight strokes, three valleys descending left-to-right. Reads as a workflow stepping down through stages. The final upstroke is the steepest, giving a "rising out" finish.
